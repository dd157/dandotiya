Professional Summary

Accomplished Mechanical Engineering faculty and researcher with 15+ years of experience (11+ post-PhD) specializing in Battery Thermal Management Systems (BTMS), Phase Change Materials (PCM), refrigeration technologies, nanofluids for CO₂ capture, and machine learning applications in thermal systems. Led funded projects worth ₹24.8 lakhs (SERB-DST PI), secured 3 granted patents + 1 published, published 21 journal articles (14 in SCIE/Q1-Q2), 28 conference papers, 2 book chapters. Google Scholar metrics: h-index 11, 428 citations. Supervised 3 completed PhDs + 3 ongoing. Strong teaching, administrative, and outreach record. Aligned with UGC 2018 norms for Associate Professor promotion/recruitment (research score >75, PhD guidance, independent funding, high-impact publications).
